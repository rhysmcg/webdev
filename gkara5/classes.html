Hatha yoga traditional yoga path that uses the body to work towards enlightenment
flex yoga a slower style that involves holding poses for a minute or more to improve flexibility and increase circulation in the joints
Restorative yoga A style that involves holding poses for a longer time with props to create space for the body to heal
Power Yoga is a vigorous, fitness-based approach to Vinyasa-style Yoga that emphasises strength and flexibility.
