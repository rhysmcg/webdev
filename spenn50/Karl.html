Karl

Breed

Savannah Cat

Age

2 Months

Weight

1.5kg/3.3lbs

Temperament

Calm, Gentle, Affectionate

Description

Karl is an adorable and serene Savannah kitten with a striking appearance and a gentle personality! With his unique coat pattern and bright eyes, he brings warmth to everyone he meets. Karl loves to lounge in cozy spots and observe his surroundings, making him a sweet little companion.

Personality

Karl is full of calm energy and tenderness! He enjoys gentle playtime and quiet cuddles, making him a perfect addition to any peaceful home. His affectionate nature ensures he gets along well with both people and other pets.

Health

Karl is healthy, up-to-date on vaccinations, and microchipped. He’s ready to find his forever home!

Ideal Home

Karl would thrive in a tranquil household that can provide affection and a relaxing environment. He’s looking for a family that can offer plenty of love and gentle interaction.

Adoption Fee and Details:

$300 (includes vaccinations and microchip)
