Cow

Breed

Bernese Mountain Dog

Age

8 Years

Weight

40kg/88lbs

Temperament

Gentle, Loyal, Affectionate

Description

Cow is a sweet and gentle Bernese Mountain Dog with a heart of gold! With his striking coat and warm eyes, he brings comfort and joy to everyone he meets. Cow loves leisurely strolls and snuggling up with his favorite people, making him an ideal companion.

Personality

Cow is calm and loyal, always ready to offer affection and companionship. He enjoys relaxing at home and loves to be included in family activities. His friendly nature ensures he gets along well with children and other pets.

Health

Cow is healthy, up-to-date on vaccinations, and microchipped. He’s ready to find his forever home!

Ideal Home

Cow would thrive in a loving household that can provide a relaxed environment. He’s looking for a family that appreciates gentle companionship and can offer plenty of affection.

Adoption Fee and Details:

$150 (includes vaccinations and microchip)

If you're interested in meeting Cow or would like more information, please follow these steps. He’s ready to find his loving forever home!

1. Contact our shelter at 0429437392
2. We will arrange a date for you to meet Cow at either the foster carer's home or a nearby park.
3. If both sides agree, you can proceed to adopt your new family member! A two-week trial begins on the day of adoption.
