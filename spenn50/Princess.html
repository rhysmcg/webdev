Princess

Breed

German Shepherd

Age

3 Years

Weight

39kg/88lbs

Temperament

Intelligent, Loyal, Protective

Description

Princess is a loyal and intelligent German Shepherd looking for her forever home. With her striking coat and keen eyes, she is sure to make a wonderful companion. Princess enjoys daily walks and loves to play with her favorite toys.

Personality

Princess is known for her protective nature and strong bond with her family. She is great with children and other pets, making her an ideal family dog. She is eager to learn and thrives on training, ready to pick up new skills!

Health

Princess is spayed, up-to-date on vaccinations, and microchipped. She’s in excellent health and eager for a new beginning.

Ideal Home

Princess would thrive in an active home where she can receive plenty of exercise and mental stimulation. She loves being part of the family and would be a perfect addition to anyone looking for a loyal companion.

Adoption Fee and Details:

$200 (includes vaccinations, spay/neuter, and microchip)

If you're interested in meeting Princess or would like more information, please follow these steps. She’s ready to find her loving forever home!

1. Contact our shelter at 0429437392
2. We will arrange a date for you to meet the pet at either the foster carer's home, nearby park or your home.
