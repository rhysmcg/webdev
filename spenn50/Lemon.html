Lemon

Description

Lemon is a playful and adventurous Dwarf hamster who loves to explore! With her bright fur and energetic spirit, she brings joy to everyone around her. Lemon enjoys climbing and running in her habitat, making her a lively companion.

Personality

Lemon is full of energy and curiosity! She loves interactive playtime and is always ready for a little adventure. Her friendly nature makes her easy to handle and a delightful addition to any family.

Health

Lemon is healthy, up-to-date on vaccinations, and ready to find her forever home!

Ideal Home

Lemon would thrive in a fun and stimulating environment where she can explore and play. She’s looking for a family that can provide plenty of attention and engaging activities.
