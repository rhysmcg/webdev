Lacie

Breed

Golden Retriever

Age

10 Years

Weight

30kg/66lbs

Temperament

Energetic, Playful, Friendly

Description

Lacie is a lively and enthusiastic Golden Retriever with a heart full of joy! With her beautiful coat and sparkling eyes, she brings happiness to everyone around her. Lacie loves to run, play fetch, and enjoy outdoor adventures, making her a fun-loving companion.

Personality

Lacie is bursting with energy and excitement! She enjoys interactive playtime and is always ready to join in on family activities. Her friendly nature ensures she gets along well with children, other dogs, and even cats.

Health

Lacie is healthy, up-to-date on vaccinations, and microchipped. She’s ready to find her forever home!

Ideal Home

Lacie would thrive in an active household that can provide plenty of exercise and stimulation. She’s looking for a family that loves outdoor adventures and can offer lots of affection.

Adoption Fee and Details:

$200 (includes vaccinations and microchip)

If you're interested in meeting Lacie or would like more information, please follow these steps. She’s ready to find her loving forever home!

1. Contact our shelter at 0429437392
2. We will arrange a date for you to meet Lacie at either the foster carer's home or a nearby park.
3. If both sides agree, you can proceed to adopt your new family member! A two-week trial begins on the day of adoption.
