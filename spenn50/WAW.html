Who are We?

Executive Director: Liam Shaw

Liam Shaw is the owner and founder of Furry Friends Adoption Centre. He has over a decade of experience in animal welfare. Passionate about rescue operations, he leads the shelter’s strategic initiatives and community outreach programs, ensuring every animal finds a loving home.

Michael Chen - Operations Manager

Michael oversees the daily operations of the shelter, including animal care and facility management. With a background in veterinary science, he ensures that all animals receive the highest standards of care and rehabilitation.

Sophia Martinez - Marketing and Communications Director

Sophia is a dynamic storyteller with a knack for digital marketing. She creates engaging campaigns to raise awareness about the shelter’s mission and promotes adoption events, utilizing social media to connect with the community.

David Lee - Fundraising Coordinator

David is an experienced fundraiser who develops and implements innovative fundraising strategies. His passion for animals drives him to forge partnerships with local businesses and organize events that support the shelter's financial needs.

Tina Rodriguez - Volunteer Coordinator

Tina is dedicated to building a strong volunteer network. She recruits, trains, and manages volunteers, fostering a supportive environment that enhances the shelter’s operations while providing a fulfilling experience for volunteers.

Dr. Emily Tran - Veterinary Services Director

Dr. Tran is a licensed veterinarian with a specialization in shelter medicine. She oversees all medical care for the animals, ensuring they receive proper vaccinations, treatment, and spay/neuter services before adoption.

Mark Sullivan - Adoption Program Manager

Mark is responsible for the adoption process, from screening potential adopters to conducting home visits. His friendly approach helps match families with their perfect pet, promoting successful and lasting adoptions.

Furry Friends Adoption Centre acknowledges Traditional Owners of Country throughout Australia and recognises the continuing connection to lands, waters and communities. We pay our respect to Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander cultures; and to Elders past, present and emerging.
