Meteor

Breed

Domestic Shorthair

Age

9 Months

Weight

3.5kg/5.5lbs

Temperament

Playful, Feisty, Affectionate

Description

Meteor is an energetic kitten with a sparkling personality! With his striking fur and bright eyes, he brings joy to everyone he meets. Despite his missing leg, Meteor loves to chase toys and explore his surroundings, making him a lively companion.

Personality

Meteor is full of playful energy and a bit of feistiness! He enjoys interactive playtime but may show his spirited side during play. With patience and understanding, he can be a loving addition to any family.

Health

Despite his missing leg, Meteor is healthy, up-to-date on vaccinations, and microchipped. He’s ready to find his forever home!

Ideal Home

Meteor would thrive in a household that can provide structure and understanding. He’s looking for a family that can engage him with plenty of playtime while respecting his spirited nature.

Adoption Fee and Details:

$150 (includes vaccinations and microchip)

If you're interested in meeting Meteor or would like more information, please follow these steps. He’s ready to find his loving forever home!

1. Contact our shelter at 0429437392
2. We will arrange a date for you to meet Meteor at either the foster carer's home or a nearby park.
3. If both sides agree, you can proceed to adopt your new family member! A two-week trial begins on the day of adoption.
