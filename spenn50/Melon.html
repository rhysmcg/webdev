Description

Melon is an adorable and playful Syrian hamster with a lively spirit! With her soft fur and bright eyes, she brings joy to everyone who meets her. Melon loves exploring her surroundings and playing with toys, making her a delightful little companion.

Personality

Melon is full of curiosity and energy! She enjoys interactive playtime and loves to be handled gently. Her affectionate nature ensures she becomes a favorite member of any family.

Health

Melon is healthy, up-to-date on vaccinations, and ready to find her forever home!

Ideal Home

Melon would thrive in a cozy environment where she can explore and play. She’s looking for a family that can provide a safe habitat and plenty of love and attention.
