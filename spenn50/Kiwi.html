Kiwi

Breed

Dwarf Hamster

Age

8 Months

Weight

50g/0.11lbs

Temperament

Energetic, Playful, Friendly

Description

Kiwi is a charming and energetic Dwarf hamster with a zest for life! With her soft fur and inquisitive nature, she brings joy to everyone she meets. Kiwi loves to explore her habitat and enjoys interactive playtime, making her a delightful companion.

Personality

Kiwi is full of energy and curiosity! She loves running on her wheel and playing with toys, and she’s always ready for a little adventure. Her friendly demeanor makes her easy to handle and a great addition to any family.

Health

Kiwi is healthy, up-to-date on vaccinations, and ready to find her forever home!

Ideal Home

Kiwi would thrive in a cozy environment where she can explore and play. She’s looking for a family that can provide a safe habitat and plenty of love and attention.
