Description

Grant is a charming and relaxed British Shorthair with a dignified presence! With his plush coat and striking eyes, he brings a sense of calm to every room. Grant enjoys lounging in sunny spots and observing the world around him, making him a wonderful companion.

Grant is independent yet affectionate! He enjoys his quiet time but will gladly curl up beside you for some gentle petting. His easygoing nature makes him a perfect fit for a cozy home.

Grant is healthy, up-to-date on vaccinations, and microchipped. He’s ready to find his forever home!
