Blue

Breed

Common Ostrich

Age

11 Months

Weight

45kg/99lbs

Temperament

Curious, Playful, Friendly

Description

Blue is a vibrant and energetic young ostrich with a lively spirit! With his striking feathers and curious nature, he brings joy to everyone who encounters him. Blue loves to explore his surroundings and enjoy playful interactions, making him a delightful companion.

Personality

Blue is full of curiosity and playfulness! He enjoys engaging with people and is always ready for a little adventure. His friendly demeanor ensures he gets along well with other animals and loves to be part of the action.

Health

Blue is healthy, up-to-date on vaccinations, and ready to find his forever home!

Ideal Home

Blue would thrive in a spacious environment that allows for exploration and play. He’s looking for a family that can provide plenty of attention and enrichment in a safe setting.

Adoption Fee and Details:

$500 (includes vaccinations and health check)
