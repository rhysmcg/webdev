Description

Banana is a sweet and gentle Dwarf hamster who loves a cozy environment! With her soft fur and timid nature, she brings a sense of calm to those around her. Banana enjoys quiet time in her habitat and will flourish with gentle handling and care.

Personality

Banana is a bit shy but incredibly affectionate once she feels comfortable! She enjoys exploring at her own pace and appreciates a peaceful atmosphere. Her gentle demeanor makes her a wonderful companion for a loving family.

Health

Banana is healthy, up-to-date on vaccinations, and ready to find her forever home!

Ideal Home

Banana would thrive in a calm environment where she can feel secure. She’s looking for a family that can provide patience, love, and a cozy habitat for her to explore.
