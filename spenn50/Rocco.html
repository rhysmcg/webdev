Description

Rocco is a vibrant and lively young crocodile with a fascinating personality! With his striking appearance and inquisitive nature, he brings excitement wherever he goes. Rocco enjoys basking in the sun and exploring his environment, making him a captivating companion.

Personality

Rocco is full of energy and curiosity! He loves to interact and is always eager to explore new surroundings. His playful demeanor makes him a unique addition to any suitable home.

Health

Rocco is healthy, up-to-date on vaccinations, and microchipped. He’s ready to embark on a new adventure with a caring owner.

Ideal Home

Rocco would thrive in a spacious environment that allows for exploration and enrichment. He’s looking for an experienced owner who can provide a secure habitat and appreciate his captivating nature.
