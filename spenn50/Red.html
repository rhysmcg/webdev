Red

Breed

Caiman

Age

3 Years

Weight

11.3kg/25lbs

Temperament

Curious, Playful, Affectionate

Description

Red is a spirited and friendly caiman with a charming personality! His bright eyes and playful nature bring joy wherever he goes. Red loves to bask in the sun and explore his surroundings, making him an adventurous companion.

Personality

Red is full of curiosity and energy! Always eager to interact, he enjoys playful activities and exploring new environments. His sociable nature makes him a delightful addition to any family.

Health

Red is healthy, up-to-date on vaccinations, and microchipped. He’s ready for a new beginning in a loving home.

Ideal Home

Red would thrive in an active household that can provide space for him to roam and explore. He loves being part of the family and is looking for a dedicated owner who appreciates his unique charm.

Adoption Fee and Details:

$150 (includes vaccinations and microchip)

If you're interested in meeting Red or would like more information, please follow these steps. He’s ready to find his loving forever home!

1. Contact our shelter at 0429437392
2. We will arrange a date for you to meet Red at either the foster carer's home, a nearby park, or your home.
3. If both sides agree, you can proceed to adopt your new family member! A two-week trial begins on the day of adoption.
