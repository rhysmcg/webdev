Prancer

Breed

Beagle Mix

Age

3 Years

Weight

11.3kg/25lbs

Temperament

Energetic, Enthusiastic, Affectionate

Description

Prancer is a spirited and affectionate beagle mix with a heart of gold! With his expressive eyes and wagging tail, he brings joy wherever he goes. Prancer loves to explore the great outdoors and would be thrilled to join you on hikes, walks, or even just lounging in the backyard .

Personality

Prancer is full of energy and enthusiasm! He’s always ready for a game of fetch or a fun adventure. His playful nature is matched only by his sweet disposition. Prancer gets along well with other dogs and adores people, making him a perfect family companion.

Health

Prancer is spayed, up-to-date on vaccinations, and microchipped. He’s in good health and ready for a fresh start.

Ideal Home

Prancer would thrive in an active household where he can get plenty of exercise and playtime. He loves being part of the family and would be a wonderful addition to anyone looking for a loyal companion.

Adoption Fee and Details:

$150 (includes vaccinations, spay/neuter, and microchip)

2. We will arrange a date for you to meet the pet at either the foster carer's home, nearby park or your home.
