About Cityscape Realty

At Cityscape Realty, we believe that finding your dream home should be an exciting and stress-free experience. As a premier real estate agency in Brisbane, our mission is to connect buyers and sellers with top-tier properties that meet their unique needs and lifestyles.

Whether you are looking for a cozy condo, a family home, or a luxury property, we offer a wide range of options in the best neighborhoods across the city. Our dedicated team of real estate professionals is committed to guiding you through every step of the process, ensuring that your property journey is smooth and successful.

Meet the Founder - Vanessa Collins

Vanessa Collins is the visionary behind Cityscape Realty. With over 15 years of experience in the real estate industry, Vanessa has developed a deep understanding of what makes a property truly special. She founded Cityscape Realty with the goal of helping people find homes that aren't just investments, but spaces where memories are made.

Having worked in various aspects of real estate, from residential sales to property management, Vanessa's passion lies in helping clients discover properties that align with their lifestyle and aspirations.
